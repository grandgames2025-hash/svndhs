About DHS

The Department of Homeland Security is a federal agency dedicated to protecting the nation from threats and ensuring the security of our borders, infrastructure, and communities.

Our mission is to defend the United States against terrorism, manage our borders, enforce immigration laws, and provide disaster relief assistance.

Our Divisions

🚁

Air & Marine

Protects our borders through air and maritime surveillance and enforcement operations.

🚗

Border Patrol

Monitors and secures our national borders against illegal crossing and contraband.

🔍

Investigation

Conducts federal investigations to identify and prevent threats to national security.

💳

Customs

Regulates and facilitates international trade while enforcing customs laws.

🏥

Emergency Management

Prepares for, responds to, and recovers from disasters and emergencies.

🔐

Cybersecurity

Protects critical infrastructure and government systems from cyber threats.
